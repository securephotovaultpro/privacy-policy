Privacy Policy for Fidelity Card V2

Effective date: March 30, 2026

Fidelity Card V2 is designed with privacy in mind. The application operates entirely offline and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or sensitive user data.

Data Collection and Usage

The App does not collect any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

All data entered into the App (including fidelity cards, barcodes, and settings) is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device.

Internet and Data Transmission

The App does not require an internet connection and does not communicate with any external servers.

Device Permissions

  • Camera (`CAMERA`): Used solely to scan barcodes and QR codes from your fidelity cards.
  • Storage: Used only to save your card data and images locally on your device.

Data Sharing

Since the App does not collect or transmit any user data, no data is shared, sold, or disclosed to third parties.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising SDKs.

Contact
