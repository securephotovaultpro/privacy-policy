Privacy Policy for Fidelity Card V2

Effective date: March 30, 2026

Fidelity Card V2 respects your privacy. This App is designed to help you manage your fidelity cards locally and securely. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to any external servers.

Information Collection and Use

The App does not collect personal information (such as your name, email, or phone number). All card data, barcodes, and settings are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to any cloud service.

Camera and Media

The App accesses your device's Camera only to allow you to scan barcodes and QR codes from your physical fidelity cards. It may also access your gallery if you choose to import a card image. This data remains entirely on your device.

Permissions Required

  • Camera Access (`CAMERA`): Required to scan and digitize your physical fidelity cards.
  • Microphone Access (`RECORD_AUDIO`): Note: This permission may be included by the camera library but is not used for recording.
  • Storage Access: Required to save card data and images locally on your device.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or trade your data with any third parties. There are no advertising or analytics trackers integrated into the App.
